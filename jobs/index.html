The roadmap to your career success

You’re in control. We’re with you along the way. A career path is comprised of a sequence of jobs that make up a career plan. A career plan includes short-term or long-term goals leading to an ideal career, while a career path includes the jobs that step an individual towards your goals and objectives.

  • Step 1. Select your career path and register on our website.

    Choose your from IT (PHP, .NET developer), Finance and Accountancy and learn about what it takes to land your first job offer.

  • Step 2. Meet with a career mentor to work out an action plan for your next goal

    Our experienced career mentor will work closely with you to work out the skill gap, and a plan to get you there. An action plan sometimes invovles professional training, social functions and professional development workshops.

  • Step 3. Your mentor will keep track of your progress towards your career goal.

    Built-in checkpoints in the action plan enables our mentor keep track of your progress. If you get stuct, your mentor is just an email away.

  • Step 4. Attending professional development courses to learn the hard skills you will need

    Attend our professional training courses, e.g. CFA exam training, PHP programming bootcamp. We recognise, while soft skills are important, outstanding hard skills win you the offer.

  • Step 5. Your mentor will look for potential job/internship opportunities for you

    Many companies recruits through us because we are more than a recruitment agency - we also develop candidates' skills required for the position. We will keep on eye on potential opportunities for you.

  • Step 6. When you close to complete your action plan, your mentor lodges job applications for you.

    Job application is a daunting and sometimes frustrating process. Let us do the job for you - from crafting your resume, tailoring your cover letter to editing your response to selection criteria. We will help you every step along the way.

  • Step 7. You attend interviews and wait for the outcome

    Get ready for interview opportunities raining down. With our mock interview and interview bootcamp training, you will surely nail your next interview.
